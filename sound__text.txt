												A music festival is a gathering where various 
												musicians and bands come together to perform 
												live music for an audience. These events can 
												span over a few days and often feature multiple 
												stages with different genres of music playing 
												simultaneously. Festivals offer a diverse range 
												of musical acts, from mainstream pop and rock to 
												indie, electronic, jazz, and more niche genres. 
												Attendees typically purchase tickets to gain 
												access to the festival grounds, where they can 
												enjoy performances, food, and various activities.
