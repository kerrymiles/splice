About us

Company Spline, located in the heart of Scotland, is one of the leading innovators in the field of sound recording. Not only does it provide high-quality sound recording services, but also allows its customers to distribute music through its own platform. This opens up to musicians doors to a world of great possibilities, allowing them to achieve to a wide audience and strengthen its presence in the industry.

Spline's three main leaders — these are people with deep knowledge in music and technology that together create synergies that contribute to the growth and innovation. Their combined efforts have resulted in the creation of a unique platforms where every aspect of the music process — from recording to distribution – is handled with the utmost care and professionalism.

Spline strives to make every customer feel part of the a large and creative family. The company offers a wide range of services, including studio recording, mastering, mixing, and post-production. In addition, Spline is actively working to expand its portfolio, including new and innovative services, such as the creation of sound designs and musical accompaniment for multimedia projects.

With its platform, Spline provides musicians with more than just instruments to disseminate their creativity, but also analytical data that help understand audience preferences and optimize marketing strategies. This allows artists to interact with their listeners and build a successful career.
